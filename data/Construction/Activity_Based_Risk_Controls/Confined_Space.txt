Title: Confined Space
Path: Project > Construction > Activity-Based Risk Controls > Confined Space
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Activity-Based Risk Controls

Overview
Controls entry into permit-required or non-permit confined spaces with isolation, monitoring, and rescue readiness.

Typical data / fields
- space ID
- classification
- hazards present
- atmospheric monitoring results
- isolation status
- entrants, attendant, and permit state

Primary owners
- Superintendent
- Foremen
- Project safety manager

When it is used or reviewed
Reviewed daily to weekly and revised whenever field conditions, sequencing, or crews change.

Common outputs / evidence
- completed record
- required approvals
- follow-up actions
