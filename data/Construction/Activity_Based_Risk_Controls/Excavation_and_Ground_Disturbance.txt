Title: Excavation & Ground Disturbance
Path: Project > Construction > Activity-Based Risk Controls > Excavation & Ground Disturbance
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Activity-Based Risk Controls

Overview
Controls trenching, buried utilities, soil stability, access, and spoil placement during ground disturbance.

Typical data / fields
- location
- depth
- utility clearance status
- soil type
- protective system
- competent person inspection and permit number

Primary owners
- Superintendent
- Foremen
- Project safety manager

When it is used or reviewed
Reviewed daily to weekly and revised whenever field conditions, sequencing, or crews change.

Common outputs / evidence
- completed record
- required approvals
- follow-up actions

- Work at Height
- Confined Space
