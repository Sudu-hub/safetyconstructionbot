Title: Lifting & Rigging
Path: Project > Construction > Activity-Based Risk Controls > Lifting & Rigging
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Activity-Based Risk Controls

Overview
Controls lifting operations, rigging selection, signaling, and load stability during field work.

Typical data / fields
- lift ID
- load description
- weight and center of gravity
- rigging gear selection
- lift plan approval
- signal person and pre-lift check

Primary owners
- Superintendent
- Foremen
- Project safety manager

When it is used or reviewed
Reviewed daily to weekly and revised whenever field conditions, sequencing, or crews change.

Common outputs / evidence
- completed record
- required approvals
- follow-up actions

Related items
- Work at Height
- Confined Space
- Electrical Safety
