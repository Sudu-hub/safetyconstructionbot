Title: Work at Height
Path: Project > Construction > Activity-Based Risk Controls > Work at Height
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Activity-Based Risk Controls

Overview
Controls elevated work through access selection, fall prevention/protection, rescue planning, and inspection.

Typical data / fields
- task area
- height or exposure
- access method
- fall protection system
- rescue plan
- competent person inspection status

Primary owners
- Superintendent
- Foremen
- Project safety manager

When it is used or reviewed
Reviewed daily to weekly and revised whenever field conditions, sequencing, or crews change.

Common outputs / evidence
- completed record
- required approvals
- follow-up actions
