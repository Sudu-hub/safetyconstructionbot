Title: Electrical Safety
Path: Project > Construction > Activity-Based Risk Controls > Electrical Safety
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Activity-Based Risk Controls

Overview
Manages energized work risk, arc flash boundaries, lockout needs, and equipment condition in the field.

Typical data / fields
- equipment ID
- voltage or energy level
- arc flash or shock boundary
- lockout points
- qualified persons
- test and authorization results

Primary owners
- Superintendent
- Foremen
- Project safety manager

When it is used or reviewed
Reviewed daily to weekly and revised whenever field conditions, sequencing, or crews change.

Common outputs / evidence
- completed record
- required approvals
- follow-up actions

- Confined Space
