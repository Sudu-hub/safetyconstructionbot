Overview
Tracks lagging and leading indicators so leadership can see safety performance and intervene quickly.

Included topics
- TRIR / DART Tracking
- Open vs Closed Findings
- Near Miss Trends
- Leading Indicator Dashboard

Typical data / fields
- workstream owner
- scope summary
- review cadence
- open actions
- linked child deliverables
- completion status

Primary owners
- Project safety manager
- Project controls
- Leadership team

When it is used or reviewed
Reviewed daily to weekly and revised whenever field conditions, sequencing, or crews change.
