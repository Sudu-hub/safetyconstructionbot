Overview
Measures backlog and closure performance for inspections, audits, and corrective actions.

Typical data / fields
- period
- total findings
- open count
- closed count
- aging buckets
- closure rate and owner

Primary owners
- Project safety manager
- Project controls
- Leadership team

When it is used or reviewed
Reviewed daily to weekly and revised whenever field conditions, sequencing, or crews change.

Common outputs / evidence

- required approvals
- follow-up actions
