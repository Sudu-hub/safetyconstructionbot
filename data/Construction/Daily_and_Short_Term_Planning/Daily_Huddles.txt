Title: Daily Huddles
Path: Project > Construction > Daily & Short-Term Planning > Daily Huddles
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Daily & Short-Term Planning

Overview
Brief shift-start meetings that align the crew on work plan, changing conditions, and critical controls.

Typical data / fields
- date and shift
- attendees
- planned work
- top risks
- weather or site changes
- action items

Primary owners
- Foremen
- Superintendent
- Safety coordinator

When it is used or reviewed
Reviewed daily to weekly and revised whenever field conditions, sequencing, or crews change.

Common outputs / evidence
- completed record
- required approvals
- follow-up actions

Related items
- Pre-Task Plans
- Job Hazard Analysis (JHA)
- Permit to Work
