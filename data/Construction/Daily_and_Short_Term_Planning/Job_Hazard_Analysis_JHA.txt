Title: Job Hazard Analysis (JHA)
Path: Project > Construction > Daily & Short-Term Planning > Job Hazard Analysis (JHA)
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Daily & Short-Term Planning

Overview
Breaks a job into steps and assigns hazards and controls to each step before work proceeds.

Typical data / fields
- job steps
- hazards by step
- controls
- PPE and tools
- responsible person
- review date and crew acknowledgement

Primary owners
- Foremen
- Superintendent
- Safety coordinator

When it is used or reviewed
Reviewed daily to weekly and revised whenever field conditions, sequencing, or crews change.

Common outputs / evidence
- review findings
- action list
- approval or sign-off

Related items
- Pre-Task Plans
- Daily Huddles
- Permit to Work
