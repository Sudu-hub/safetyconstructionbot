Title: Permit to Work
Path: Project > Construction > Daily & Short-Term Planning > Permit to Work
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Daily & Short-Term Planning

Overview
Provides formal authorization for high-risk construction activities when required conditions and approvals are met.

Typical data / fields
- permit type
- location
- scope
- hazards
- isolations or tests
- issuing authority, start/end time, and closeout status

Primary owners
- Foremen
- Superintendent
- Safety coordinator

When it is used or reviewed
Reviewed daily to weekly and revised whenever field conditions, sequencing, or crews change.

Common outputs / evidence
- completed record
- required approvals
- follow-up actions

Related items
- Pre-Task Plans
- Daily Huddles
- Job Hazard Analysis (JHA)
