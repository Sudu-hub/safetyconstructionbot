Title: Pre-Task Plans
Path: Project > Construction > Daily & Short-Term Planning > Pre-Task Plans
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Daily & Short-Term Planning

Overview
Defines task scope, crew, hazards, controls, and resources immediately before work begins.

Typical data / fields
- task description
- crew list
- hazards
- controls
- equipment and PPE
- stop-work triggers and sign-off

Primary owners
- Foremen
- Superintendent
- Safety coordinator

When it is used or reviewed
Reviewed daily to weekly and revised whenever field conditions, sequencing, or crews change.

Common outputs / evidence
- approved plan or strategy
- revision history
- communication or sign-off record

Related items
- Daily Huddles
- Job Hazard Analysis (JHA)
- Permit to Work
