Title: Incident Reports
Path: Project > Construction > Incident & Near-Miss Management > Incident Reports
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Incident & Near-Miss Management

Overview
Formal records of injuries, property damage, environmental releases, or significant unwanted events.

Typical data / fields
- incident ID
- date and time
- location
- people involved
- event summary
- classification and immediate actions

Primary owners
- Project safety manager
- Project leadership
- Supervisors

When it is used or reviewed
Reviewed daily to weekly and revised whenever field conditions, sequencing, or crews change.

Common outputs / evidence
- final report or summary
- supporting attachments
- approval record

Related items
- Root Cause Analysis
- Corrective Action Plans
- Lessons Learned Repository
