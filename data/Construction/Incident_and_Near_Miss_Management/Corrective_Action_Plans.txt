Title: Corrective Action Plans
Path: Project > Construction > Incident & Near-Miss Management > Corrective Action Plans
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Incident & Near-Miss Management

Overview
Assigns structured actions to eliminate causes, track ownership, and prevent recurrence.

Typical data / fields
- CAP ID
- linked finding
- action description
- owner
- due date
- priority, closure evidence, and effectiveness check

Primary owners
- Project safety manager
- Project leadership
- Supervisors

When it is used or reviewed
Reviewed daily to weekly and revised whenever field conditions, sequencing, or crews change.

Common outputs / evidence
- approved plan or strategy
- revision history
- communication or sign-off record

Related items
- Incident Reports
- Root Cause Analysis
- Lessons Learned Repository
