Title: Construction
Path: Project > Construction
Node type: Lifecycle Phase
Parent: Project

Overview
Field execution phase where work is performed, controlled, inspected, investigated, and measured in real time.

Included topics
- Activity-Based Risk Controls
- Daily & Short-Term Planning
- Inspection & Compliance
- Incident & Near-Miss Management
- Live Performance Metrics

Typical data / fields
- phase owner
- phase objectives and entry criteria
- key risks and assumptions
- open actions and readiness status
- linked workstream deliverables
- phase review or gate date

Primary owners
- Project manager
- Superintendent
- Project safety manager

When it is used or reviewed
Reviewed daily to weekly and revised whenever field conditions, sequencing, or crews change.

Common outputs / evidence
- Field control records
- Inspection logs
- Incident investigations
- Live KPI dashboards
