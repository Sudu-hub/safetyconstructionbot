Title: Environmental Monitoring Logs
Path: Project > Construction > Inspection & Compliance > Environmental Monitoring Logs
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Inspection & Compliance

Overview
Captures environmental readings and checks such as dust, noise, water, and waste controls.

Typical data / fields
- parameter monitored
- date and time
- location
- reading or result
- threshold or limit
- corrective action and follow-up

Primary owners
- Project safety manager
- Supervisors
- Inspectors

When it is used or reviewed
Reviewed daily to weekly and revised whenever field conditions, sequencing, or crews change.

Common outputs / evidence
- completed log entries
- exception list
- trend-ready data
