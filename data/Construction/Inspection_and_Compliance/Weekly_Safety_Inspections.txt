Title: Weekly Safety Inspections
Path: Project > Construction > Inspection & Compliance > Weekly Safety Inspections
Node type: Topic / Deliverable

Overview
Recurring broad inspections of work areas, housekeeping, access, signage, and safe work practices.

Typical data / fields
- inspection date
- area
- inspector
- findings
- severity
- action owner, due date, and closure status

Primary owners
- Project safety manager
- Supervisors
- Inspectors

When it is used or reviewed
Reviewed daily to weekly and revised whenever field conditions, sequencing, or crews change.

Common outputs / evidence
- completed record
- required approvals
- follow-up actions
