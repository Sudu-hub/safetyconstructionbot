Title: Equipment Inspection Logs
Path: Project > Construction > Inspection & Compliance > Equipment Inspection Logs
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Inspection & Compliance

Overview
Records the condition and readiness of tools, mobile equipment, and critical safety devices.

Typical data / fields
- equipment ID
- inspection date
- inspector
- condition items
- defects
- tag status and repair action

Primary owners
- Project safety manager
- Supervisors
- Inspectors

When it is used or reviewed
Reviewed daily to weekly and revised whenever field conditions, sequencing, or crews change.

Common outputs / evidence
- completed log entries
- exception list
- trend-ready data

Related items
- Weekly Safety Inspections
- Scaffold & Fall Protection Audits
- Environmental Monitoring Logs
