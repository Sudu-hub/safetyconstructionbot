Title: EMR & Incident History Review
Path: Project > Preconstruction > Trade Partner Qualification > EMR & Incident History Review
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Trade Partner Qualification

Overview
Checks Experience Modification Rate (EMR) and past incident performance to gauge loss history and stability.

Typical data / fields
- EMR values by year
- OSHA or recordable case history
- lost-time and DART results
- claims trend narrative
- benchmark comparison
- reviewer comments

Primary owners
- Procurement lead
- Preconstruction manager
- Safety director

When it is used or reviewed
Prepared before mobilization and revisited whenever design assumptions, site constraints, or scope change.

Common outputs / evidence
- review findings
- action list
- approval or sign-off

- Insurance & Compliance Verification
