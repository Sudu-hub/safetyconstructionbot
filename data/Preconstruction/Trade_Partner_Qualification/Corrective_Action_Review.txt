Title: Corrective Action Review
Path: Project > Preconstruction > Trade Partner Qualification > Corrective Action Review
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Trade Partner Qualification

Overview
Reviews unresolved findings and prior corrective actions to confirm recurring issues are being addressed.

Typical data / fields
- prior finding or CAP ID
- issue summary
- root cause or theme
- action owner
- due date
- closure evidence and reopen status

Primary owners
- Procurement lead
- Preconstruction manager
- Safety director

When it is used or reviewed
Prepared before mobilization and revisited whenever design assumptions, site constraints, or scope change.

Common outputs / evidence
- review findings
- action list
- approval or sign-off

Related items
- Safety Prequalification Review
- EMR & Incident History Review
- Insurance & Compliance Verification
