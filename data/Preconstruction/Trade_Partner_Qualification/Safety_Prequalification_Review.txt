Title: Safety Prequalification Review
Path: Project > Preconstruction > Trade Partner Qualification > Safety Prequalification Review
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Trade Partner Qualification

Overview
Reviews a prospective trade partner's safety program, staffing, and training readiness before award.

Typical data / fields
- company profile
- safety program elements
- competent person coverage
- training matrix
- past citations or enforcement history
- review score and approval status

Primary owners
- Procurement lead
- Preconstruction manager
- Safety director

When it is used or reviewed
Prepared before mobilization and revisited whenever design assumptions, site constraints, or scope change.

Common outputs / evidence
- review findings
- action list
- approval or sign-off

Related items
- EMR & Incident History Review
- Insurance & Compliance Verification
- Corrective Action Review
