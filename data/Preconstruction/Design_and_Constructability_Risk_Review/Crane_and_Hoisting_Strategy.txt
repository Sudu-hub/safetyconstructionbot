Title: Crane & Hoisting Strategy
Path: Project > Preconstruction > Design & Constructability Risk Review > Crane & Hoisting Strategy
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Design & Constructability Risk Review

Overview
Defines lifting plans, crane selection, load paths, pick zones, and coordination rules for hoisting work.

Typical data / fields
- crane type and capacity
- critical picks
- load weights and radii
- rigging plans
- pick paths and exclusion zones
- operator or rigger qualification status

Primary owners
- Design manager
- Operations representative
- Safety lead

When it is used or reviewed
Prepared before mobilization and revisited whenever design assumptions, site constraints, or scope change.

Common outputs / evidence
- approved plan or strategy
- revision history
- communication or sign-off record

- Temporary Works Risk Planning
- Hazard Elimination Workshops
