Title: High-Risk Scope Identification
Path: Project > Preconstruction > Design & Constructability Risk Review > High-Risk Scope Identification
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Design & Constructability Risk Review

Overview
Pinpoints scopes with high energy, severe consequence potential, complex sequencing, or simultaneous operations.

Typical data / fields
- scope item
- hazard type
- trigger conditions
- inherent risk rating
- planned controls
- responsible party and review date

Primary owners
- Design manager
- Operations representative
- Safety lead

When it is used or reviewed
Prepared before mobilization and revisited whenever design assumptions, site constraints, or scope change.

Common outputs / evidence
- completed record
- required approvals
- follow-up actions
