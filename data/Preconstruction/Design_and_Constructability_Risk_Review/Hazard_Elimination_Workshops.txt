Overview
Facilitates workshops to remove or reduce hazards through design changes, sequencing, or prefabrication.

Typical data / fields
- workshop date
- participants
- hazards reviewed
- elimination or substitution ideas
- decisions made
- follow-up actions and status

Primary owners
- Design manager
- Operations representative
- Safety lead

When it is used or reviewed
Prepared before mobilization and revisited whenever design assumptions, site constraints, or scope change.

- follow-up actions
