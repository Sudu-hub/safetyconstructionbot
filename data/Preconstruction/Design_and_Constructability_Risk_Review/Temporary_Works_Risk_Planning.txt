Title: Temporary Works Risk Planning
Path: Project > Preconstruction > Design & Constructability Risk Review > Temporary Works Risk Planning
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Design & Constructability Risk Review

Overview
Plans risks for temporary structures and systems such as shoring, formwork, falsework, and access platforms.

Typical data / fields
- temporary works item
- design basis and load assumptions
- engineer of record
- inspection and hold points
- permit needs
- erection and removal plan

Primary owners
- Design manager
- Operations representative
- Safety lead

When it is used or reviewed
Prepared before mobilization and revisited whenever design assumptions, site constraints, or scope change.

Common outputs / evidence
- approved plan or strategy
- revision history
- communication or sign-off record
