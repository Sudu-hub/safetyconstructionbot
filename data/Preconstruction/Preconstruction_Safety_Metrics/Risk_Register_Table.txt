Title: Risk Register (Table)
Path: Project > Preconstruction > Preconstruction Safety Metrics > Risk Register (Table)
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Preconstruction Safety Metrics

Overview
Consolidates identified safety risks, ratings, owners, controls, and follow-up actions before work starts.

Typical data / fields
- risk ID
- description
- cause
- consequence
- inherent rating
- controls, owner, and residual rating

Primary owners
- Preconstruction manager
- Cost or controls manager
- Safety lead

When it is used or reviewed
Prepared before mobilization and revisited whenever design assumptions, site constraints, or scope change.

Common outputs / evidence
- completed record
- required approvals
- follow-up actions

- Training Plan Matrix
