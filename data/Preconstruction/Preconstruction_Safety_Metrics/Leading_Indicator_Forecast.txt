Title: Leading Indicator Forecast
Path: Project > Preconstruction > Preconstruction Safety Metrics > Leading Indicator Forecast
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Preconstruction Safety Metrics

Overview
Predicts proactive safety activities such as inspections, training, observations, and engagement efforts.

Typical data / fields
- indicator name
- baseline
- forecast quantity
- target period
- owner
- assumptions and variance notes

Primary owners
- Preconstruction manager
- Cost or controls manager
- Safety lead

When it is used or reviewed
Prepared before mobilization and revisited whenever design assumptions, site constraints, or scope change.

Common outputs / evidence
- KPI summary
- trend view
- management review notes

Related items

- Training Plan Matrix
