Overview
Assigns funds to staffing, training, protective equipment, monitoring, and contingency safety needs.

Typical data / fields
- budget line item
- planned amount
- phase or cost code
- justification
- approved amount
- forecast variance

Primary owners
- Preconstruction manager
- Cost or controls manager
- Safety lead

When it is used or reviewed
Prepared before mobilization and revisited whenever design assumptions, site constraints, or scope change.

Common outputs / evidence

- required approvals
- follow-up actions
