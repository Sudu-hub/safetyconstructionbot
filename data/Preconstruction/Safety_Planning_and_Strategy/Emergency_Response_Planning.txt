Title: Emergency Response Planning
Path: Project > Preconstruction > Safety Planning & Strategy > Emergency Response Planning
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Safety Planning & Strategy

Overview
Defines alarms, contacts, routes, resources, medical response, drills, and recovery steps for emergencies.

Typical data / fields
- credible emergency scenarios
- response roles and contact list
- nearest medical and public emergency resources
- alarms, routes, and muster points
- drill schedule and lessons learned
- emergency equipment inventory

Primary owners
- Project safety lead
- Project manager
- Superintendent

When it is used or reviewed
Prepared before mobilization and revisited whenever design assumptions, site constraints, or scope change.

Common outputs / evidence
- approved plan or strategy
- revision history
- communication or sign-off record

Related items
- Site-Specific Safety Plan
- Environmental Risk Assessment
- Logistics Risk Modeling
