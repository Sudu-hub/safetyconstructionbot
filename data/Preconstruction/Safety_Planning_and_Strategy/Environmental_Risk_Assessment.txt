Title: Environmental Risk Assessment
Path: Project > Preconstruction > Safety Planning & Strategy > Environmental Risk Assessment
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Safety Planning & Strategy

Overview
Evaluates potential impacts to air, water, soil, waste, noise, and nearby receptors before work begins.

Typical data / fields
- environmental aspect and impact register
- sensitive receptors and site constraints
- permits and applicable limits
- control and mitigation measures
- spill response and waste strategy
- residual risk rating

Primary owners
- Project safety lead
- Project manager
- Superintendent

When it is used or reviewed
Prepared before mobilization and revisited whenever design assumptions, site constraints, or scope change.

Common outputs / evidence
- review findings
- action list
- approval or sign-off

Related items
- Site-Specific Safety Plan
- Emergency Response Planning
- Logistics Risk Modeling
