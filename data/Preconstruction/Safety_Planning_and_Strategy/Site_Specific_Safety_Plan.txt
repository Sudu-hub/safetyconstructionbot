Title: Site-Specific Safety Plan
Path: Project > Preconstruction > Safety Planning & Strategy > Site-Specific Safety Plan
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Safety Planning & Strategy

Overview
Defines project-specific rules, roles, hazard controls, emergency contacts, and procedures for the site.

Typical data / fields
- project name and location
- revision number and approval date
- safety organization and contact list
- site rules, PPE requirements, and restricted areas
- high-risk work controls and permit rules
- emergency information and muster details

Primary owners
- Project safety lead
- Project manager
- Superintendent

When it is used or reviewed
Prepared before mobilization and revisited whenever design assumptions, site constraints, or scope change.

Common outputs / evidence
- approved plan or strategy
- revision history
- communication or sign-off record

Related items
- Emergency Response Planning
- Environmental Risk Assessment
- Logistics Risk Modeling
