Title: Preconstruction
Path: Project > Preconstruction
Node type: Lifecycle Phase
Parent: Project

Overview
Early-phase planning used to identify hazards, qualify partners, and design safety into the work before mobilization.

Included topics
- Safety Planning & Strategy
- Trade Partner Qualification
- Design & Constructability Risk Review
- Preconstruction Safety Metrics

Typical data / fields
- phase owner
- phase objectives and entry criteria
- key risks and assumptions
- open actions and readiness status
- linked workstream deliverables
- phase review or gate date

Primary owners
- Preconstruction manager
- Design manager
- Project safety director

When it is used or reviewed
Prepared before mobilization and revisited whenever design assumptions, site constraints, or scope change.

Common outputs / evidence
- Approved readiness plans
- Qualified trade partner list
- Design risk actions
- Baseline safety metrics and budget
