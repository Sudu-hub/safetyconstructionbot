Title: Hazard Escalation Logs
Path: Project > Maintenance > Incident & Service Events > Hazard Escalation Logs
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Incident & Service Events

Overview
Tracks hazards that were identified in maintenance and escalated for higher-level decision or shutdown.

Typical data / fields
- hazard ID
- date raised
- description
- risk level
- escalation path
- interim controls and resolution status

Primary owners
- Maintenance manager
- EHS lead
- Operations manager

When it is used or reviewed
Used during routine operations, task execution, scheduled maintenance cycles, and periodic audits.

Common outputs / evidence
- completed log entries
- exception list
- trend-ready data

Related items
- Service Incident Reports
- Maintenance CAP Tracking
