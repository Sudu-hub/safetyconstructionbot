Overview
Composite measure of asset safety risk based on condition, consequence, exposure, and control strength.

Typical data / fields
- asset or system
- condition score
- consequence score
- exposure frequency
- control effectiveness
- index value and risk band

Primary owners
- Reliability engineer
- Maintenance manager
- EHS lead

When it is used or reviewed
Used during routine operations, task execution, scheduled maintenance cycles, and periodic audits.

Common outputs / evidence

- trend view
- management review notes
