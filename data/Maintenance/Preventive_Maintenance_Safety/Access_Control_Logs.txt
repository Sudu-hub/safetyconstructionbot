Title: Access Control Logs
Path: Project > Maintenance > Preventive Maintenance Safety > Access Control Logs
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Preventive Maintenance Safety

Overview
Records who entered controlled areas, when, and under what authorization.

Typical data / fields
- date and time
- person
- controlled area
- authorization type
- escort requirement
- access method and exit time

Primary owners
- Maintenance supervisor
- Operations supervisor
- EHS lead

When it is used or reviewed
Used during routine operations, task execution, scheduled maintenance cycles, and periodic audits.

Common outputs / evidence
- completed log entries
- exception list
- trend-ready data

Related items
- Lockout/Tagout (LOTO)
- Permit to Work (Ops)
- Maintenance JHA
