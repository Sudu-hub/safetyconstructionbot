Title: Permit to Work (Ops)
Path: Project > Maintenance > Preventive Maintenance Safety > Permit to Work (Ops)
Node type: Topic / Deliverable
Parent: Preventive Maintenance Safety

Overview
Operations-issued permit authorizing maintenance tasks under facility operating rules.

Typical data / fields
- permit number
- work scope
- area or system
- isolations
- gas test or readings
- issuer/receiver, duration, and handback

Primary owners
- Maintenance supervisor
- Operations supervisor
- EHS lead

When it is used or reviewed
Used during routine operations, task execution, scheduled maintenance cycles, and periodic audits.

Common outputs / evidence
- completed record
- required approvals
- follow-up actions

Related items
- Lockout/Tagout (LOTO)
- Access Control Logs
- Maintenance JHA
